Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Jarvis Insta ("the App") handles information.

1. Data Collection

The App does not collect, store, or share any personal data from users. It operates solely on behalf of its owner for personal content publishing.

2. TikTok API Usage

The App uses the TikTok Content Posting API to publish videos to the owner's TikTok account. No data is stored beyond what is necessary for authentication (access tokens stored locally).

3. Third-Party Services

The App interacts with TikTok's API in accordance with TikTok's Privacy Policy and Developer Terms.

4. Data Security

All authentication tokens are stored locally and never transmitted to any third party.

5. Contact

For privacy questions: quyotee@live.de
